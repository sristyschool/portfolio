Chairman Speech

Assalamu Alaikum,
My inherent place is Tangail. Sristy Education Family had started its journey here from the 5th June 1993, taking pledge to serve the country, aim is to spread education all over the country. Gradually our aim is on the way of fulfilling. Students of all the districts of Bangladesh are captivating the opportunity of education flair of Sristy and its educational service is now at home and abroad. Sristy Education Family has already passed 27 years with highly reputation in education sector in Bangladesh.

Teaching System of Sristy

  • In our residential system which is known as The model of Bangladesh, in where the students of 64 districts are taking ideal model of educational system
  • In our academic building, there has residential facilities with modern controlling system.
  • For getting maximum A+, We are providing extra classes besides our schedule classes.
  • We have an experienced residential teacher for every 15 students whose main duty is to make them understood their studies and take what lesson has been given for the next day.
  • We have also a strong monitoring system that is consisted by our chief of residential system and assistant super.
  • We are to supply pure drinking water, hygienic and nutritious food every day.
  • There are also availability of CC Camera, Lift, all time security system
  • We are also providing here Daily Newspapers, Television (Under controlled), facilities of indoor and outdoor games, visiting historical places, with the help of residential teachers.

Special Aspects of Sristy

  • Regular teaching with Multimedia/ Projector
  • Excessive class in Math, English, Science, Physics and Chemistry.
  • Teaching in participatory system to make easy creative subject.
  • Enough MCQ Test and Model Test, taking daily lessons and regular practical class
  • Taking weekly problems solve class and weekly exam of all subjects.
  • There is Extra Care for little talented and inattentive students.
  • Realistic steps have been taken to select subject based weak students, to identify the reasons and to remove it.
  • There has Brain storming system to make students busy in study, to create inventive power and competitive sense.
