Chairman Speech

Assalamu Alaikum,

My inherent place is Tangail. Sristy Education Family had started its journey here from the 5th June 1993, taking pledge to serve the country, aim is to spread education all over the country. Gradually our aim is on the way of fulfilling. Students of all the districts of Bangladesh are captivating the opportunity of education flair of Sristy and its educational service is now at home and abroad. Sristy Education Family has already passed 27 years with highly reputation in education sector in Bangladesh. Having 17 educational institutions in all over the country and a huge number of young, energetic, dynamic & skilled manpower working here, Sristy Education Family has brought about a revolutionary change in education sector in Bangladesh. By the time Sristy has elaborated its branches in Dhaka, Rajshahi, Khulna, Gazipur, Ashulia, Jamalpur, Sirajganj and Sherpur along with Tangail by the permission of Education Board. These educational institutions have created a high level of responses among education lovers. From these educational institutions, our students are getting more admissions into public universities after completing HSC. British and National Curriculum (English version) are being taught at Sristy now. Our institutions are conducted by modern technology to keep pace with the modern world for your children.

From the next session our institutions are going to have a very big plan of enlarging our activities on the whole country. Insha-Allah we will be able to spread the light of education through the establishment of our planned academic institutions broadly. We wish we shall get your helpful hands in the future for establishing our dream plans “Sristy Medical College & Hospital, Sristy University of Bangladesh and Sristy Institute of Technology”. May Allah bless us all and grant all our wishes for these great efforts. All the students, parents, well-wishers and collaborators of Sristy are the main inspirers for the successful establishments of our institutions and academic projects. Sristy serves the nation with spirit. For more information please visit -www.sristy.edu.bd

-Dr. Shariful Islam Ripon, PMJF
